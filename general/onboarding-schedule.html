Week 1: Introduction

You will have a 30 minute meeting with the organizer.

In this meeting, you will talk about yourself.

You will also talk about what you like and what team you would like to join.

You will get an invited to join Trello.

This is where you will write a bit about what you like and about youself.

Week 2: Meet Your Team Member

You will have a 60 minute meeting with the team leader.

In this meeting, you will learn about how we work together.

You will also learn how you will work with your team leader.

You will get an invite to join Project27's WhatsApp community.

You will receive a document with 10 different tasks.

Look through these tasts and decide which one you want to do.

Week 3: Pick Your First Task

You will have a 60 minute meeting with your team leader.

In this meeting, you will talk about the task from week 2.

You will choose your first task to start.

If you are unsure, your team leader will help you choose.

Then, you will start working on your task.

Week 4: Feedback and Transition

You will have a 30 minute meeting with your team leader and the organizer.

In this meeting, you will talk about how you did on your first task from week 3.

You will also talk about your onboarding experience.

You will share how you felt working with your team leader, using the tools, and the difficulty of the tasks.
