Ways of working

  • We work on one task at a time.
    • Focusing on a single task increases efficiency and reduces errors.
    • Allows for deeper concentration and better quality work.
  • We break down complex projects into manageable tasks.
    • Each task has a clear objective and outcome.
    • Makes it easier to track progress and identify any issues early.
    • Helps to allocate resources more effectively.
  • We work in teams.
    • Collaboration leverages the strengths of each team member.
    • Encourages creative problem-solving through diverse perspectives.
    • Fosters a supportive and communicative work environment.
  • We have regular check-ins to keep on track.
    • Weekly meetings to discuss progress and any obstacles.
    • Ensures everyone is aligned with the project goals.
    • Provides an opportunity for feedback and course correction.
  • We commit a small number of hours between check-ins to complete tasks.
    • Allows for steady progress without burnout.
    • Facilitates regular review and adjustment of workloads.
    • Maintains momentum and accountability.
  • We use tools like Trello to remain accountable and transparent on progress.
    • Trello boards are updated regularly with task statuses.
    • Enables clear visibility of project timelines and deadlines.
    • Helps in identifying bottlenecks and reallocating resources if needed.
  • Everything we do has to be complementary to what everyone else is doing.
    • Ensures that all tasks contribute to the overall project goals.
    • Promotes a harmonious and efficient workflow.
    • Prevents overlap and duplication of effort.
